Products

Want to draw attention to your business?! There’s no limit—you imagine it and we’ll create a T-shirt or other garment. Maybe you want to cheer on your team and favorite player. Or want to promote your company at a trade show or other event. We can help you get your message on a shirt and get you noticed. We use the latest technology in Direct-to-Garment (DTG) printing. DTG prints any image directly onto a garment or fabric without needing any set up. The ECO friendly water based ink is printed directly onto fabric which results in exceptional “soft feel” and offers more flexibility than screen-printing in terms of color variation and turn-around time (especially for smaller orders). The higher the quality of your image / design, the better the outcome will be.

Digital Printing

Digital printing has transformed printing services and allows Print + Sign Specialists to provide fast, high quality, short runs at affordable prices. Digital printing eliminates many of the mechanical steps required for conventional offset printing. Plus we can offer help with creative and design. So let us help you create a brand and make your business stand out.
