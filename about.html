About Us

Print + Sign Specialists is your one stop print and sign shop. It’s our goal to develop long term relationships with our customers by providing the best services for the lowest price with fast turnaround. Owner, Leon Odendaal brings over 30 years of experience in the printing industry. We offer the highest quality digital printing and specialize in rush delivery. No matter the quantity of printed materials you need, we are ready to help you make a great impression.

Why Use Print + Sign Specialists?

Quality: We understand that you have many options when it comes to choosing among the area printers, which is why we are constantly exploring new ways to provide the highest quality. Great Price: We offer competitive pricing and strive to always give you the highest quality at the lowest price. Service: Customer Service is our top priority. It’s our goal to build long–term partnerships with our customers. We accommodate every client with a customized (personalized?) program that works for them. Experience: Since 1996, Leon Odendaal has been involved in the print industry. Prior to owning a print shop Leon had experience repairing print and copy machines. He brings a customer service perspective along with the technical background to ensure the highest quality print product. In 2007 he purchased PDQ Printing Services in Champaign, IL and has been involved in the print industry in Champaign and Vermilion Counties since.
